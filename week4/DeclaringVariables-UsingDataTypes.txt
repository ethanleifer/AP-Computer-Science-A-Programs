in java:

int n;

or

Declaration:
  dataType variableName (optional initialization statement);

Infinity many Datatypes

Primitive Datatypes: (** are important ones)
  Numeric Primitives:
    Whole #
      byte: 2 - bit whole #'s
      short: 16 - bit whole #'s
      **int: 32 - bit whole #'s**
      long: 64 - bit whole #'s

    Decimal #
      **double: double - precision floating - point #'s**
      float: single - precision

  **boolean: true or false**
  char: single characters

Capital dataTypes are classes
Primitive dataTypes are lowercase

Restriction on int is [-2^31,2^31-1]
Restriction on long is [-2^63,2^63-1]

Round off error is unavoidable
